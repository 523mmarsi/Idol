Charlotte North

Charlotte North is an attacker on the Boston College Lacrosse Team. She is an incredible lacrosse player who has broken numerous records. She is ranked #2 on NCAA College Women's Lacrosse D1 Individual Stats. She's a 2021 NCAA National Champion and was named the Most Outstanding Player of the tournament. Charlotte North was the female winner of the 2021 Tewaaraton Award. She also broke the single season record for goals with 102 on the year. She won the Mary Garber ACC Female Athlete of the Year Award (across the board in the ACC, not just lacrosse), set the NCAA Tournament goals record with 31, tied the NCAA Championship goals record with 6. She is an absolutely insane player. She also got the chance to throw out the first pitch at the Rangers game on August 17, 2021.

  • From all of the accomplishments listed above, anyone could understand why she is my idol. It is because she is an absolute beast and inspiration. She has created a huge name for herself. She is one of the most known and best women's lacrosse players. She also known for her insane trickshots that she successfully finishes.
